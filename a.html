Ming-Style Sofa

An improved product of Ming-style Official Hat Chair. The traditional Official Hat Chair restrains the behavior of the person to a certain extent and requires the person to sit in a positive position. Ming-style Sofa is more comfortable and fashion while retaining Chinese style.
