Diagnopal

Diagnosis + pal

Aiding network medical treatment and enables people to perform self-diagnosis at home.

Overview

Problem (Brief)

Network Hospital has come out and patients can see a doctor by video. For the meanwhile, stethoscope a basic tool for diagnosis for doctors but it is not user-friendly for the people without medical training. Therefore, I want to improve existing diagnostic products.

The ‘Right’Problem and INSIGHTS:

After research, I found that the ideal product should-- 1. Meet the needs of daily life. 2.Can be moved it easily and operated comfortably in the process of using. 3. Suitable for individual use and diagnosis for others. 4. Consider women's body shape, so that data can be captured more accurately in the process.

SOLUTION:

Diagnopal is equipped with a combination of high resolution camera, infrared basal thermometer, digital stethoscope. Diagnopal meets doctors' need for most conventional examination tools. People can use Diagnopal under guidance and move it easily and operate comfortably because of AI driven insights and data analytics. AI-analyzed diagnosis result will be shown to users in moments, and ready to be sent to doctor for further treatment.
