Interactive Box

In this project, I treat the used waste as idiosyncratic, symbolic, and expressive materials that depict the diversity of tensions between human, and its consumption of artifacts and nature.

Development

Iteration-1

I drew a picture of human behaviors on the abandoned discs. According to the distance of audience, the wooden stick behind will strike at different degrees, and the discs will reflect light to different degrees.

Feedback: The showing effect is not good.

Iteration-2

I still used discs, but changed the structure.

Feedback: In addition to light and color, I should look for more interaction possibilities.

Iteration-3

I drew people’s facial features on several pieces of iron plates and hang some rings on them. According to the distance of the audience, the iron plates rotates at  different rates, and the rings make different sounds by knocking.
