Moving Cabinets

The small cabinet elements, which can be moved, flexibly adjust to suit any chosen use, providing the storage space that happens to be needed.

System

Cabinet is departed into several units, which makes house-moving much easier. This function can help the elder and the children to adjust the improper height of cabinet by pressing the bottom or using an app on the phone.

Technique

What is the principle of it? Linear motor technology in magnetic suspension! Cabinets can be no longer stick to the wall, but can finish the vertical and transverse motions.When the cabinet moves horizontally, the two well passes through the track system, so that it connects the cabinet to slide from one well to the other well, so as to achieve circular operation.
